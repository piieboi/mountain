## 6. Termination

### 6a. Requesting Termination
All data can be removed if your account is in good standing with Luaily Productions. After initiating the termination process, you have 30 days to request to download your data, after which the data will be removed from all our systems. To terminate your account, contact: [luailyart+delete@protonmail.com](mailto:luailyart+delete@protonmail.com?subject=Project%20Mountain:%20Delete%20ID) with the subject line "Project Mountain: Delete ID", or simply go to our website and start a "Mountain ID Deletion Ticket."

### 6b. Definition of Good Standing
"Good Standing" refers to an account which has not been found to be in violation of our Terms of Service (ToS) or our End User License Agreement (EULA).   

Additionally, no user whom works with, is part of, is currently, or has been previously employed by a corporation which creates and or develops military weapons and/or technology, a federal agency, a police agency, or an immigration enforcement agency, can be deemed by us, as "in good standing." Therefore, all individuals known to be associated with any of the previously mentioned agencies hereby forfeit their ability to request or demand their information to be deleted.
